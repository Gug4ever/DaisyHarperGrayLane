About Daisy

A traveller at heart, a reader by nature.

The Woman Behind the Stories

Daisy Harper Gray-Lane has always been curious about everything — people, places, stories, the world in all its complexity. A traveller at heart, a reader by nature, she is the kind of person who finds something remarkable in every life she encounters.

That curiosity became a conviction: the most extraordinary women in history have never been defined by a single thing. They dared. They persisted. They changed the world — often quietly, always completely.

Brilliant Women, Remarkable Stories was born from that belief. A series of accessible, beautifully crafted biographies for young readers, it brings to life the women who shaped our world — told the way they always should have been.

Daisy lives in London with her family. She writes wherever life takes her.
